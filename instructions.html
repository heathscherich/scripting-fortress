Commands are separated by new lines. They are of the form "verb" "number" "noun"

Verbs

Commonly used verbs are
  • gather
  • build
  • store

Numbers

Any numeric will do
  • 10
  • 8
  • 24

Nouns

Commonly used nouns are
  • stone
  • wood
  • fish

Example: store 10 fish

Info

You have an inventory. It can hold 10 items. If you need more than 10 items, you will have to use a shed. Some things are automated for convenience, such as storing resources during gathering, emptying the farm, or eating when your hunger is below 50%. To end your current list of commands, type in STOP or stop and hit execute.

Shed

Holds up to 100 resources.
Costs 10 wood to build

Range

Cooks items.
Costs 20 wood and 20 stone to build

Farm

Grows seeds into vegetables. Uses plant command to plant stored seeds
Costs 15 seed and 15 wood to build.

Mine

Place to mine stone having a chance to contain metals
Costs 10 wood and 100 stone to build.

Smithy

Creates weapons or armor. Command looks like `smith 1 copper weapon`
Costs 10 iron and 100 stone to build.
